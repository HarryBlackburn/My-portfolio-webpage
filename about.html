About me,

My name is Harry Archer Blackburn and I live in Manchester. I started studying web development in 2017 - Back then I chose JavaScript as a starting language but I hoped to broaden my knowledge to other languages and technologies as I progressed. I practiced JS problem solving through Code Wars and this website is where I utilised HTML and CSS. I had an app idea similar to Shazam but for vinyl that I wanted to developing to increase my breadth of knowledge.(but Discogs beat me too it! :D) Before I went on my coding journey I worked at an IT services provider on the Northern Public Sector team (Universities/Healthcare/Government) where I worked on projects from initial stake-holder kick-offs, tenders, installations; sign offs and aftercare. (QUOTE from 2017) : "The time has come for me to move on!..." and I was right now as i was then. The time has come to move on and update my 2017 dreams. It's been an interesting full circle in the fact that now i work on a digital delivery team as a PMO Analyst where i enjoy flaunting my forgotten (at the time of making this website) skills of user engagement/financial analysis/ and generally gettting about the workplace. This was a role where i originally wanted to be before i started codinf, but the the skills have been very helpful and transferable.
